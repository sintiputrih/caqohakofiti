Ty’s ambitions, on the other hand, did not materialise to their full potential, and he was forced to relocate to Texas. Herndon rose to fame as the lead vocalist for the Tennessee River Boys, a band that performed in amusement parks across the United States in the early 1980s. Diamond Rio was the moniker given to the band when it was renamed later on. Herndon ultimately quit the band and went on to compete in the reality television show “Star Search” in 1985.

After releasing the lead single, Herndon followed up with the release of the album of the same name.
