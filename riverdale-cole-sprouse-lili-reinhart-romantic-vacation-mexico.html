Cole Sprouse and Lili Reinhart On Romantic Getaway In Mexico

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 17, 2024

Riverdale co-stars Cole Sprouse and Lili Reinhart aren't hiding their relationship anymore, and that means sharing photos from their romantic getaways on Instagram.

Related Story

The pair is currently vacationing in Mexico, meaning that they skipped Coachella altogether to spend some quality time together. And instead of hiding out, Sprouse decided to share some gorgeous photos of Reinhart on the beach, making it clear that their romance is still going strong.

Staying at Cuixmala resort, both Sprouse and Reinhart shared videos on their Instagram Stories while horse riding together:

The actor shared sweet photos of Reinhart wearing an ethereal white maxi dress by Elena Makri on the beach. The sweet series of Instagrams beach show just idyllic their getaway is—and how into each other this couple still is.

The actors unofficially confirmed their romance when they were spotted kissing in Paris last month. But in interviews, Reinhart and Sprouse continue to play it cool, not giving anything away.

Appearing at the Riverdale panel at PaleyFest 2018 last month, a fan in the audience asked if they were dating. Other cast members on stage shot each other awkward looks, but Sprouse simply replied, "No comment," which only seemed to stoke audience excitement.

Both Reinhart and Sprouse are intensely private about their lives—back in October 2017, Reinhart wrote on her Tumblr, "It’s horrifying how invested some people are in my love life. Emphasis on 'my.' It is mine. It is private. If a stranger’s love life is causing you anger, frustration or anxiety… please reevaluate your priorities."
