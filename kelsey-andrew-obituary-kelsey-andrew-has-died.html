Kelsey Andrew Obituary, Kelsey Andrew Has Died

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 24, 2024
0SHARESShareTweet

Kelsey Andrew Obituary, Death Cause – It is with overwhelming sorrow that we would like to inform our patients and community that Kelsey Andrew, our RMT, passed away on Friday, July 14th. ⁣⁣After speaking with her mom today, she informed us that she always referred to Cavan Chiropractic as her happy place. She hugged Dr. Bonner this past Monday and thanked her for the employment opportunity saying how much she loved coming to work. ⁣⁣Kelsey was a new graduate of Fleming College who hit the ground running in her career.

She was a team player, a wonderful daughter and friend. Our hearts are broken. We appreciate your understanding during this time as our staff mourn this tragic loss. ⁣⁣Kelsey’s influence extended beyond her professional and philanthropic endeavors. She was an integral part of her community, actively engaging in efforts to foster unity, inclusivity, and social progress. Whether it was through organizing community events, championing environmental causes, or advocating for the rights of marginalized groups.

Kelsey consistently demonstrated her unwavering commitment to building a better world for all. Her impact on the community will continue to be felt for years to come. In addition to her remarkable achievements, Kelsey will be remembered for her infectious laughter, radiant smile, and warm-hearted nature. She had a unique ability to connect with people from all walks of life and make them feel valued and appreciated. Kelsey’s compassion knew no bounds, and her genuine care for others resonated deeply with those fortunate enough to have known her.
