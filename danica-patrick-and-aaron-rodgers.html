Danica Patrick And Aaron Rodgers: Are They Still Together? Where Is She Now? Boyfriend And Net Worth

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

Danica Patrick And Aaron Rodgers: Are They Still Together? Former professional race car driver Danica Patrick is, pardon the pun, back on track after a difficult split. If you’re wondering where Danica Patrick is these days, she was recently seen in an episode of Running Wild with Bear Grylls. To understand more about the race car driver, read the entire story.

Danica Patrick is a retired American professional race car driver. She is the most successful female driver in American open-wheel racing history, having won the Indy Japan 300 in 2008, the only event in which a woman has competed.

She began racing at the age of ten and swiftly went to fame, winning the World Karting Association Grand National Championship three times in the mid-1990s.

Let us learn more about Danica Patrick’s whereabouts and take a closer look at her boyfriend and kids.

Danica Patrick And Aaron Rodgers: Are They Still Together?

On a recent episode of Running Wild With Bear Grylls, the retired driver and current podcast host and winemaker reflected on the end of her last relationship. Though she didn’t mention her former partner by name, it seems the man in question was Green Bay Packers quarterback Aaron Rodgers, whom Patrick dated for two years.

“I think we learn the most about ourselves through relationships, but there’s nothing like heartbreak to really throw you in the deep end of that,” Patrick told host Grylls, according to People. “I’ve learned a lot, and as broken open as I was on the sad end, I have felt so much joy in so many more instances and so many more unlikely places than I ever have, so it’s like my heart got broken open to both ends of the spectrum.”

She also praised her family for being there for her during her breakup, saying, “[My dad is] always super proud of me. My mom too. My sister…I’m really fortunate that I have a family that’s super supportive. You gotta have someone you can fall apart with, you know?”

But Danica Patrick isn’t jumping into anything quite so quickly. She’s waiting for that perfect fit. Her next partner is “going to be an extremely high-quality person,” she said on a talk-show appearance in March, per Yahoo!. “It’s not as though there are boxes to check, it’s just that I know what I want now and I’m not willing to compromise, bend as much, right?”

Where Is Danica Patrick Now?

Danica Patrick now has retired as a professional race car driver and has started working as an actress.

Patrick’s final race was the 2018 Indianapolis 500. But she could not complete the race as she crashed in the 68th lap.

Despite starting at the 7th position, she was ranked 30th at the end of the race due to her crash in the mid-race.

She is now a proud owner of a wine brand, Somnium. Danica has also appeared in several television shows and movies as of now. She made a cameo appearance in Charlie’s Angels as a driving instructor according to her Wikipedia profile.

Danica has not made a public appearance lately as she could be busy with her business ventures and philanthropic works.

Who Is Danica Patrick Boyfriend Carter Comstock?

Danica Patrick is currently dating her boyfriend Carter Comstock as of 2022.

The couple has been going on since 2021 and made the relationship Instagram official on April 17, 2021, by posting a picture of Carter kissing her on the head. She captioned the post as, “Beach workouts are fun for one, but better with you… ”

Since the couple made their relationship public, they are seen spending a lot of time together and sharing their happy moments with their followers on Instagram.

Patrick’s boyfriend Carter is co-owner at Freshly Inc. He has been with the company since 2012 and also acts as the Chief Innovation Officer of the firm.

He graduated from the University of Arizona, Eller College of Management earning his Bachelor’s degree in Business Management.

Does Danica Patrick Have Kids?

Danica Patrick does not have any kids as of now.

She has been married once but does not have any children yet.

Patrick was married to Don Trail in 2005 but they decided to end their marriage in 2013.

She was also in a relationship with NASCAR driver Ricky Stenhouse Jr. from 2012 to 2017 and NFL Quarterback Aaron Rodgers from 2018 to 2020 but she did not share any children with her past boyfriends.

What Is Danica Patrick Net Worth 2022?

Danica Patrick’s net worth is estimated at $80 million as of 2022 by Celebrity Net Worth.

She has amassed her fortune with her very successful career as a professional race car driver.
