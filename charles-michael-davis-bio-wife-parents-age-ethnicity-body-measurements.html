Age – Charles Rise to Fame

Charles Michael Davis was born on December 1, 1984 in Dayton, Ohio, USA. There is little or no information about his early life, background and education.

The Ohio Indians attended Miami University, Oxford, and Wings Model Management in Cincinnati. He started his professional career as a model after signing with talent agency Actors, Models and Talents for Christ (AMTC) .

As a model, he appeared in commercials and print ads for Nike and Footlocker. Davis’ dynamic personality was soon noticed in the television industry, and in 2005 he began playing small roles on the small screen.

Charles has appeared in a number of notable television shows which include Disney Channel That’s So Raven , where he portrayed the character of Trevor; as Liam in the 2011 ABC Family series Transferred at Birth and a recurring role as Kwan Kirkland, a quarterback for the San Diego Sabers The Game (2011). He then appeared as Marcel in The Vampire Diaries and as Dr. Jason Myers on Grey’s Anatomy . In 2013, Davis landed a starring role himself on the CW television drama, The Originals , a spin-off from The Vampire Diaries.

Davis has also acted in several Hollywood films, including The Proposal and Battle Tracks. The animator has already experienced quite a bit in his career and can easily add “director” to his CV. In 2017 he confidently switched from performer to director. Without hesitation, as inspired by Paul Wesley, Davis took control of the bulk Originals set and went behind the lens, making his directorial debut on an episode of The Originals Season Four (High Water and a Devil’s Daughter).

His parents and ethnicity

Charles Michael Davis is the son of Marina and Charles Davis. While his father is from Kentucky, USA, his mother is from Manila, Philippines. Consequently, the handsome actor has African American and Filipino roots.
