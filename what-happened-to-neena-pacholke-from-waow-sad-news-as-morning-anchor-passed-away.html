What Happened To Neena Pacholke From WAOW? Sad News As Morning Anchor Passed Away

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Neena Pacholke, a morning anchor, passed away unexpectedly. On August 27, 2022, a Saturday, she died abruptly.

On weekday mornings between 4:30 and 7 a.m. and 11 a.m., Pacholke presented the news for News 9 WAOW.

Neena Pacholke

Neena Pacholke Death Cause- What Happened To The WAOW Anchor?

The cause of Neena Pacholke’s death has not been disclosed by the WAOW. The news will be updated very shortly.

After learning about Pacholke’s passing from his family, the news station made the announcement on their official website.

Neena Pacholke, our cherished morning anchor, went away abruptly on Saturday, according to their statement.

“The whole News 9 staff is heartbroken by the loss, and we know that a great number of people are as well. Neena cherished this neighborhood and its residents. We will miss her terribly since she was a good person with a large heart and a contagious grin.”

Many of her fans have already started posting tributes to her legacy after hearing the tragic news.

In a Facebook post, Char Barr stated: “Really, really sad. Praying for the kin of channel 9 news anchor Neena Pacholke.”

Dave Kallaway left a comment on the WAOW story, writing: “Every time we crossed paths with Neena, it was like coming face to face with the sun. People in radio and television are like a club or a family. A truly remarkable broadcaster and a joy to know. We are praying for the News 9 team and her family.”

Who Is Neena Pacholke? Her Wikipedia

News anchor Neena Pacholke was well-known. She is a native of Tampa, Florida.

At her high school and college, Pacholke played competitive basketball for over ten years. The University of South Florida then awarded the newscaster a Mass Communication/Media degree.

Pacholke joined WAOW in 2017, according to her LinkedIn profile. She spent the first two years of her career as a reporter and multimedia journalist. She then received a promotion to Morning News Anchor, where she has worked for the past three years.

Pacholke worked a number of positions before joining WAOW at various companies. She has had positions with numerous companies, including Cumulus Media, Tampa Palms Compton Park, Moffitt Cancer Center, and Apple A Day Program.

Pacholke has also been on The Daily Show with Trevor Noah and Jimmy Kimmel Live. She had the handles @neenapacholketv on Twitter and @neenapacholketv on Instagram.

Morning Anchor Neena Pacholke’s Husband & Family

Her spouse and kids predeceased Neena Pacholke. She and her family resided in Marathon County, Wisconsin.

A week ago, Pacholke was seen using Facebook frequently. On August 23, 2022, she had blogged about the discovery of a body near Cloverland Lane in Rib Mountain.

Neena Pacholke Biography

American journalist Neena Pacholke served as a morning news anchor for WAOW 9 News. She was based in Oklahoma City. Neena spent her youth in Tampa, Florida, despite the fact that she was born in Chattanooga, Tennessee.

In May of 2017, she graduated from the University of South Florida with a bachelor’s degree in Broadcast Journalism and a minor in Leadership Studies. She then went on to pursue other opportunities. During her time at USF, she participated as a member of the women’ ball group (2013-2016).

She worked as an anchor and journalist for Florida Focus, a news brief that was shown on the local PBS-WUSF channel, during the semester while she was in her final year of college. She was given the opportunity to work as an understudy at 95.5 WPLJ in New York City during the late spring before her final year at USF.

In addition to that, she worked as a trainee for the video creation group at the Moffitt Cancer Center in Tampa. To tell you the truth, Neena was a fan of the Green Bay Packers before she moved to the badger state. Her mother’s side of the family is originally from Wisconsin, so it was only natural for her to root for the Packers.

She loves everything about north-central Wisconsin, especially all of the cafes in the area, noisy cheddar curds, and a weekend excursion into the Northwoods to relax.

In 2019, she also adopted a cat from the Portage County Humane Society who was given the name Murphie.

Neena Pacholke WAOW 9 News

In May of 2017, Pacholke became a member of the News 9 team. Since February of 2019, Neena has been serving as a co-anchor on Wake Up Wisconsin, having previously worked in the media industry as a sight and sound columnist. You may listen to Neena on Wake Up Wisconsin on weekday mornings between the hours of 4:30 and 7:00! In addition to that, she is responsible for drafting and securing the 11 o’clock report.

Neena Pacholke Death

Neena Pacholke, a much-loved morning anchor here at the news station, passed away unexpectedly on Saturday. Our entire staff here at News 9 is absolutely heartbroken by the death, just as we are sure a great many other people are feeling right now.

Both the neighborhood and the individuals who lived in it held a special place in Neena’s heart. We will miss her terribly since she was a good person with a large heart and a smile that caught everyone’s attention.

During this difficult time for all of us, we ask that you remember her family in your prayers and keep them in your thoughts. Later in the week, we will have much more information regarding the life that Neena lived and the legacy that she left. Please visit our Facebook page if you would like to express your sympathies or share some of your most treasured memories. Thank you.
