Meet Viktor Hammer, Armie Brother! His Age, Wife, Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, April 23, 2024

It’s only natural you’d want to stay out of the limelight if you’re a member of the Hammer family — featured on House of Hammer. And the disgraced actor Armie Hammer’s brother Viktor Hammer was trying hard to do so after the three-part docuseries premiered on September 2, 2022.

But none shall escape the GND’s eyes. Let’s get to know him better as this Viktor Hammer Bio proceeds.

Meet Viktor Hammer, Actor Armie Hammer Brother

Viktor Hammer is Armie Hammer’s only brother. But he isn’t a part of the Discovery+ documentary House of Hammer, even when Armie’s father, grandfather, and great-grandfather’s legacy is greatly discussed in the three-part docuseries.

But then again, the Discovery Plus series isn’t about the Hammer family’s greatness. Rather, the show exposes details surrounding the s*xual abuse allegations against Viktor’s brother, Armie Hammer.

Allegedly, in early 2021, Armie texted one of his exes, “I am 100% a cannibal” and “I want to eat you.” After which, more messages began to surface over the internet and The Social Network star’s career went into freefall.

However, Armie denied all allegations and claimed that any interactions with his s*xual partners were consensual. But numerous women kept accusing him of abuse, coercion, and even ra*e, which eventually led to an LAPD investigation.

Viktor Hammer Age

Viktor Hammer was born in June 1988 to parents Michael Hammer and Dru Ann Mobley. That made him 34 years of age in 2022.

He is two years younger than his brother Armie Hammer.

Did you know: Viktor was named after his great-granduncle Victor Hammer, younger brother of Armand.

Viktor Hammer Profession

Viktor Hammer was the Vice President and Financial Advisor of the Aegis team at Morgan Stanley. There, he focused on guiding nonprofit institutions and individuals to help achieve their overall mission and goals through sustainable financial management and oversight.

Reportedly, Morgan Stanley is a financial advisory firm with its headquarters in Franklin, Tennessee. It had offices in 732 locations and a total of 26,500 people on staff. Remarkably, it also administered $1 trillion in assets between its 2,129,899 client accounts, making it one of the largest investment advisory firms in the nation by assets under management.

But over Viktor’s 11 years of experience as a financial advisor, he has journeyed with companies such as Armand Hammer Foundation and Morgan Stanley Private Bank, and National Association as well.

His expertise includes Financial planning services, Portfolio management for individuals and small businesses, Portfolio management for investment companies, Portfolio management for businesses or institutional clients, Pension consulting services, Selection of other advisers, Educational seminars/workshops, and more.

He is also well versed in Research, Microsoft Excel, Customer Service, Microsoft Word, Mergers and Acquisitions, Mutual Funds, Administrative Assistants, and Bauxite.

As for his qualifications, Viktor has a B.S. in Business Administration from Pepperdine University and a Masters in Business Administration, MBA, with an academic emphasis in Finance, from the University of North Carolina Chapel Hill.

Also, he owned a Series 66 license, qualifying him as both a securities agent and an investment advisor representative. The  Series 66 license was approved to advise clients in California, South Carolina, Tennessee, and Texas.

How Much Is Viktor Hammer Net Worth?

Viktor Hammer garnered a net worth of above $5 million by 2022.

As a finance advisor, he was estimated to earn around $78 thousand per annum. However, his fee structure also depended on the Percentage of Assets and Commissions.

But most of his net worth is inherited. The Hammer family was left with $40 million after the battle for Armand’s estate ensued.

Viktor Hammer Wife

Viktor Hammer married his wife Angelia De Meistre on August 31, 2013, at  Montecito estate with Pure Joy Catering in Summerland, CA.

According to Pure Joy Catering, their wedding had a wonderful full High Tea with all the trimmings touched off with Pimm’s Cups and Gin Rickeys, perfect refreshing light summer cocktails, little fresh scones, house-made jam, devon cream, and adorable tea sandwiches.

The lovebirds first met during their summer after a junior year at Pepperdine University in a Spanish class and fell for one another. It was Viktor who made the first move and asked Angelia for a date at the Jonathan Club in Santa Monica.

Talking more about Viktor’s wife, Angie holds an M.A. in Marriage and Family Therapy from Lipscomb University. And as of 2022, she worked as an “M.MFT, LMFT” at Ready Nest Counseling, and at The Refuge Center for Counseling for over 2 years each.

Also, she is a  member of the AAMFT, a certified Level I Restoration therapist, and has received training in maternal mental health with “Postpartum Support International and Mental Health First Aid” with the National Council for Behavioral Health.

Trivia: Viktor and his wife adopted a god named Juno from a shelter in Ventura County.

Viktor Hammer Height

Viktor Hammer stands tall at a height of around 6 feet 1 inch (185 cm).
