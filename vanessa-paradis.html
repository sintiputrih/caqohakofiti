Vanessa Paradis is a French singer, model, songwriter, actor, and voice artist who gained popularity at a young age. She has released albums and tracks like Joe le taxi, Les Sources, Variations sur le même t’aime, Divinidylle, Bliss, Vanessa Paradis, Love Songs, Just as Long as You Are There, Les espaces et les sentiments, Il y a, Dès que j’te vois, Dis-lui toi que je t’aime, and M&J. She has also branched out as an actor and has been a part of projects including The Magic Roundabout, My Angel, Café de Flore, Sous les jupes des filles, Frost, Knife + Heart, Fading Gigolo, Yoga Hosers, Girl on the Bridge, Lost in La Mancha, Witch Way Love, A Monster in Paris, and La clef.
