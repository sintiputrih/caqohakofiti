Acting Career

Julianne Moore’s acting career has spanned over several decades, with her talent and versatility shining through in a wide range of roles. She began her journey with television appearances and off-Broadway theater, gaining recognition for her work on the soap opera “As the World Turns.” Moore then transitioned to the big screen, captivating audiences with her compelling performances in acclaimed films.

One of Moore’s breakthrough roles came in the 1993 film “Short Cuts,” directed by Robert Altman. Her portrayal of Marian Wyman earned her critical acclaim and put her on the map as a rising star in Hollywood. She continued to impress audiences with her performances in movies such as “Boogie Nights,” “The Hours,” and “Still Alice,” for which she won the Academy Award for Best Actress.
