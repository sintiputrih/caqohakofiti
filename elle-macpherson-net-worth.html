Lifestyle and Vacations

Lifestyle

Macpherson follows specific diets and regimens to keep her healthy and young. She is living proof that workout, proper diet, and determination grant you a wonderful body and healthy life.

Elle sticks to an alkaline diet which recommends eating whole foods, vegetables, fruits, and plenty of water while cutting down refined sugar, meat products, fried foods, and alcohol.

She is basically a vegan, but she sometimes listens to her sweet cravings and indulges in cream pie or chocolate.

The model eats three meals a day plus 2-3 snacks depending on her need. Moreover, she takes multivitamins, probiotics, and prebiotics, all in the form of a green super powder (Super Elixir green powder).

Talking about her morning routine, she wakes up at 5 am and starts her day sipping hot water and lemon mixed with ginger or cayenne. Then, she sits for meditation. Likewise, her exercise is about an hour-long that includes either jogging or going swimming on her bike.

Vacations

Elle is among the person who loves vacation, even more, beach vacations! She has always been spotted several times spending her getaways with her family or boyfriends in different cities.

In 2019, Macpherson celebrated her 55th birthday in Utah. Also, the model shared her adventurous holiday pictures on Instagram, where she was seen trekking the canyons in a plaid shirt, cowboy hat, and bodywarmer.

Charity

Elle is a European Ambassador for RED, a foundation initiated by Bono and Bobby Shriver to fight AIDS, Tuberculosis, and Malaria. The foundation has been working together with Global Fund since 2006.

Likewise, she is an Australian Ambassador for the Smile Foundation, a non-profit organization that helps children with rare diseases. Similarly, the organization also arranges research grants.

Furthermore, in 2020, the model announced to contribute WelleCo’s 100% sales for a certain time, helping to fight the Australian bushfire.

Modeling, Acting, Investments, and Endorsements

Modeling

Her first modeling gig was in 1982 when Macpherson modeled for a commercial project by Coca-cola for the only sugar-free drink at that time, Tab Cola. Elle looked gorgeous wearing a red string bikini in the ad.

After the cola commercial, the model got her recognition, and in the next four years, she already had worked with the magazines like Vogue, Cosmopolitan, Elle, and GQ.

Similarly, Elle received further exposure from the annual Swimsuit Issue of Sports Illustrated Magazine, for which she set the record by appearing five times on their cover (1986, 1987, 1988, 1994, and 2006).

In 1989, she was entitled “The Body” by TIME Magazine. Furthermore, the model has worked for many prominent magazine covers during the 1980s, such as Harper’s Bazaar, Marie Claire, Vogue, Elle, GQ, Cosmopolitan, Tatler, Flare, Maxim, Glamour, New York, Muse, Easy Summer, and so on.

In May 1994, Macpherson was also featured in the Playboy Magazine.

Likewise, she has walked the runways for huge names like Ralph Lauren, Louis Vuitton, Christian Dior, Donna Karan, Nicole Miller, Valentino, Michael Kors, Calvin Klein, and more.

In the meantime, Elle also worked for big brands like Victoria’s Secret, H&M, Omega, Pinko, etc., in her prime modeling time.

Last but not least, Macpherson did enhance The CEO Magazine, as well. In February 2020, the model-businesswoman was featured on the famous magazine cover.

Acting and Television

Elle Macpherson started her acting career by acting in the 1994 movie Siren. She also has shared screens of the films like Batman & Robin (1997), and The Mirror Has Two Faces (1996).

Likewise, Macpherson has been a part of the famous American sitcom FRIENDS, where she played the role of a dancer by the name of Janine.

Furthermore, the model has hosted several TV shows such as NBC’s Fashion Star  (2012–2013), Britain’s Next Top Model (2010–2013), and Ireland’s Next Top Model. And, she was also the executive producer in these shows.

Likewise, she also did host an episode of Saturday Night Live (SNL).

Investments

The successful model is also a wise investor, and she has put money into several business ventures.

Elle Macpherson Inc is a company founded by Elle in 1994 that produced and sold calendars and launched workout videos.

Elle is also an owner of an international restaurant chain by the name Fashion Cafe, collaborating with three other supermodels Naomi Campbell, Christy Turlington, and Claudia Schiffer.

In 1990, MacPherson teamed with Bendon Limited Apparel to sell the lingerie range, becoming the best-selling lingerie line in the UK and Australia. Furthermore, she runs a line of skincare sold in Europe, The Body.

Eventually, the model, in 2014, launched a new skincare company by the name WelleCo. The company creates plant-based beauty supplements (skin and body care) with indigenous Australian ingredients.

Endorsements

Elle Macpherson has endorsed many brands and companies throughout her career, which helped extend her net worth. She has been known for a long time promoting Biotherm, a luxury French skincare company.

Moreover, she was also on the board of directors for Hot Tuna Clothing, an Australian clothing brand.

Apart from that, Elle co-hosted Miss Universe 2001 along with another supermodel, Naomi Campbell.

Working for numerous best-selling brands, the model has promoted them too. Some of the top brands represented by Elle are Diet Coke, Chrysler, Victoria’s Secret, and Budweiser.

Career

Elle Macpherson was born in 1964 in Killara, Australia, to her parents- Peter Gow, a one-time president of the Sydney rugby team, and Frances Gow, a nurse.

When Elle was only 10 years old, her parents divorced, and she lived with her mother and siblings. She briefly attended Sydney University to study law.

In between, she traveled to New York to spend a year doing modeling work to cover her university expenses.

Further, she signed Click Model Management in NYC, after which her modeling career started. Elle came in recognition after her Tab cola advertisement in 1982.

The ad manifested her “the girl next door” identity in Australia.

Since then, the supermodel’s career kept mounting where she landed with several ad projects, brands collaboration, acting in films/TV shows, and much more.

She is also a successful entrepreneur and has been awarded several titles for her outstanding business models such as Everywoman Ambassador Award, World Career Award, and many more.

Personal Life

Elle (21) married Gilles Bensimon, a fashion photographer, in 1986 before splitting up in 1989.

In 1996, the supermodel started dating Arpad Busson, with whom she has two sons, Arpad and Aurelius. However, the pair had their break-up in 2005.

Further, in 2013, Macpherson married her billionaire boyfriend Jeffrey Soffer, who she dated from 2009 to 2012. Unfortunately, they announced their divorce after four years of marriage.
