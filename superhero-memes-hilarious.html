By Mustafa Gatollari

Marvel studios had a novel idea that started years ago. It was a simple philosophy: instead of turning in movies they knew were going to print money just because people are familiar with these beloved comic book characters and superheroes, they asked themselves a basic question.

"What if we made these movies good?"

Years later and pretty much every major Marvel studio release has been solid. Yes, Thor: The Dark World could've been much, but try telling me with a straight face that Rangarok isn't just incredible and I'll call you a liar.

DC's trying to keep up with Marvel's excellence, and although their films are making money, it's obvious that they've got a lot of work to do when it comes to, you know, churning out quality content. Maybe these Marvel memes will inspire them to get their act together.
