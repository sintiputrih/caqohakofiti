Rashmi Desai Biography

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, July 6, 2024

Rashmi Desai is one of the popular faces of the Indian television industry that always expresses impeccable acting and always comes out with pretty expressions.

Who Is Rashmi Desai? 

She was born on February 13th, 1986, to Late Ajay Desai and Rasila Desai (Teacher) in Nagaon, Assam, India. She grew up with a younger brother. Rashmi did her schooling from Ulubari High School and J B Khot High School in Assam.

Further, she attended the Assam University to complete her graduation. Moreover, Rashmi is a trained Kathak and Bharatnatyam dancer. This classical dance training helped her to get the right poise, grace, and expressions to act in serials and films.

Movies and TV Works

Rashmi Desai (age 30) is the beautiful and the leading actress of the Television industry. She started her career with Zee TV. She played the role of Mandodari in serial ‘Raavan’. Later on, she was spotted in other serials like ‘Pari Hoon Main’, Meet Mila De Rabba and others but got fame from serial ‘Uttaran’.

Before entering on the small screen, she worked in several B-grade movies, Bhojpuri, and Assamese films. She was seen in Nach Baliye 7 along with Nandish Sandhu. She will soon be seen in Colors' daily soap Dil Se Dil Tak.

Marriage, Children

Desai belongs to Asian ethnicity and holds Indian nationality. she got married to her Uttaran co-star  Nandish Sandhu in Dholpur, in 2012. They are blessed with a baby girl. But later the couple has parted ways and divorced after four years. As per the latest reports, Rashami Desai is dating Sidharth Shukla. The two of them will be soon sharing screen space in the upcoming show, Dil Se Dil Tak.

Net Worth

Rashmi Desai earns a lot of from her career. She made her 2 million dollar fortune with Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa 5 & Uttaran. Her current net worth and salary are no Disclosed. She is also popular in social sites.
