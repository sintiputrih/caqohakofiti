Angus T. Jones Is All Grown Up See His Full-On Beard

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, January 19, 2024

Officially a full man! Two and a Half Men alum Angus T. Jones is all grown up. The former child star, now 22, looked almost unrecognizable while grabbing coffee in Venice, California, on Monday, January 4.

Jones landed the role of Jake Harper on the sitcom when he was just 10 years old. In 2010, Jones became the highest-paid child actor on TV, earning a whopping $300,000 an episode.

In 2012, Jones made headlines when he shockingly slammed the hit CBS show during a video rant posted by religious website Forerunner Chronicles. In the video, Jones pleaded with fans to “please stop watching Two and a Half Men” and to “please stop filling your head with filth.”

Jones later apologized for his remarks, explaining that he “never intended” to disrespect his colleagues or display “a lack of appreciation of the extraordinary opportunity of which I have been blessed.” Jones left the show after season 10.

Following his departure, Jones joined forces with the World Harvest Outreach Church, and since March 2014 he has been living in Colorado, where he reportedly attends the University of Colorado Boulder.
