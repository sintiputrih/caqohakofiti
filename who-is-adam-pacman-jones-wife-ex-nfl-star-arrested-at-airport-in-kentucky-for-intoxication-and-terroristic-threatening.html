Who is Adam 'Pacman' Jones' wife? Ex-NFL star arrested at airport in Kentucky for 'intoxication and

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, August 15, 2024

CONCINNATI, KENTUCKY: Adam 'Pacman' Jones, a former NFL player who married a talented businesswoman named Tishana Holmes in 2014, was arrested at a Kentucky airport for "intoxication and terroristic threatening." 

Holmes, a skilled entrepreneur from Nashville, Tennessee, supported Jones' career up until his retirement in 2019, as per The Sun.

She also shared management of her husband's company, Pac Entertainment Group.

Holmes also co-managed Jones' J24 Athletic Complex, a center for athletic training. The native of Nashville has 97,500 Instagram followers, which demonstrates her online appeal.

Her posts frequently feature pictures of her and her family as well as advertisements for the businesses she and her husband own.

Why was Adam 'Pacman' Jones arrested?

The 39-year-old, who last competed in the NFL in 2018, was arrested before takeoff after being removed from an aircraft at Cincinnati Northern Kentucky International Airport (CVG) shortly after 6 am.

Police were called to the area at around 6 am, according to numerous media sources, to deal with an "unruly passenger."

According to Captain Kevin Klute of the Boone County Jail, Jones, a regular on the 'Pat McAfee Show' on ESPN, was arrested by police for 'terroristic threatening and intoxication'.

When he spoke to reporters outside the jail after being freed later that morning, he referred to his charges as "bulls***."

"Do I look intoxicated to you?" Jones questioned a reporter, in a video shared by WKRC. "I only been locked up for two hours."

"Y'all talking about 6:30 in the morning. Who in the hell is drunk at 6:30 in the morning, man?"

Although the reason for the altercation and arrest aboard the plane is not known, Jones asked the media in attendance to "report the motherf***ing facts."

Jones was lamenting the Bengals' Sunday defeat to Cleveland in a post on social media just prior to his arrest.

Adam 'Pacman' Jones and Tishana Holmes' children

Holmes and Jones were married on July 5, 2014. Before getting married, the couple had been dating for seven years. The couple has three children together, a son and two daughters.

Jones also acquired custody of his friend Chris Henry's three children after he died in a sad accident. Henry was a former member of the Cincinnati Bengals along with Jones, reported The Sun.
