When was Stevie Wonder last child born?

Stevie Wonder welcomed his ninth born who is his last-child, Nia Morris with his girlfriend Tomeeka Robyn Bracy on December 17, 2014. Nia is 7 years old (as of 2021)

How many baby mothers does Stevie Wonder have?

Stevie Wonder has five (5) different baby mothers. He has nine (9) children with all these women. The women are Yolanda Simmons, Melody McCulley, Karen “Kai” Millard Morris, and Tomeeka Robyn Bracy. His last baby mama seems to be a private woman whose name is unknown by many.
