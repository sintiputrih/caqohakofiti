Interesting Facts You Need To Know About Rolling Ray

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, March 28, 2024

Rolling Ray is known as a rapper, social media influencer, and reality TV star, from the United States of America who came under the spotlight after appearing in MTV’s Catfish: Trolls in 2019. He has since released songs like Thank you, Gun Action, and Look At Me.

He started his music career in 2014 and is very active on Instagram with a significant fan following which he uses to promote brands.

Rolling Ray biography

Rolling Ray was born as Raymond Harper on September 5th, 1996 in Martinsburg, Missouri, United States of America, and as of 2022 he is 26 years old and currently lives in Washington DC, United States of America.

He is an American by nationality and his ethnicity is mixed. He’s gay and his zodiac sign is Virgo.

With regards to his physical appearance, he stands at a height of 5 feet 1 inch (155 cm) and weighs 62 kg (137 lbs)
