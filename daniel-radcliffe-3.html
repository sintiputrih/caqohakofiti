Daniel Radcliffe aka Daniel Jacob Radcliffe is an English actor and model. As of 2023, Daniel Radcliffe’s net worth is $113 million. He is popularly known for portraying the famous role of Harry Potter in the popular Harry Potter film series.

He is also popularly known for playing various other famous roles in several popular movies like The Woman in Black, Swiss Army Man, and The Lifespan of a Fact. Along with these, he is also a well-known philanthropist who has contributed to many charities.

Daniel Radcliffe Wiki/Biography

Born on 23 July 1989, Daniel Jacob Radcliffe’s age is 33 Years Old as of 2023. He was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family from London, England, United Kingdom. He is a British by nationality and has his belief in the Jewish religion.

Daniel Radcliffe Childhood Pic

He completed his early schooling at three-day schools for boys, Redcliffe School in Chelsea’s Redcliffe Square, Sussex House School in Chelsea’s Cadogan Square, and City of London School in London. Since his childhood, he was inclined towards acting and started acting at the young age of 10 years.

He acquired his further education through private tuitions, as he was busy in his acting projects and passed his three AS-level exams with A grades. Afterward, he took a break from education to enhance his acting career.

Career

Daniel embarked on the journey towards his career in 1999 by playing the character of a young boy in the BBC television drama “David Copperfield”. He debuted in the film in 2001 with the Irish-American spy thriller film “The Tailor of Panama”.

Director Chris Colombus, J.K. Rowling, and producer David Heyman were greatly impressed with his acting in “David Copperfield” and he grabbed a chance to appear as the lead character “Harry Potter” in the fantasy film”Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone in 2001. He has been a part of the Harry Potter series from 2001 to 2011.

In 2013 he appeared as “Allen Ginsberg” in the romantic drama film “Kill Your Darlings” and as “Wallace” in the romantic drama film “What If”. He landed the character “Yossi Ghinsberg” in the 2017 Australian biographical survival drama film “Jungle” and as “Sean” in the 2018 American crime action film “Beast of Burden”.

His other notable filming work includes “December Boys”, “The Woman in Black”, “Trainwreck”, “Victor Frankenstein”, “Now You See Me 2”, “Swiss Army Man”, “Imperium” etc. Moreover, he has also made his appearance as “Miles” in the action-comedy “Guns Akimbo” in 2019 and as “Tim Jenkin” in the thriller drama film “Escape from Pretoria” in 2020.

Daniel portrayed the character “Craig Bog” in the American anthology comedy television series “Miracle Workers” and lent his voice for a vampire “Edmund” in an episode of the American animated sitcom “The Simpsons”.
