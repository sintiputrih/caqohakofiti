Banks Violet Bair Is The Daughter Of Celebrity Hilary Duff and Matthew Koma

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 24, 2024

Being a celebrity child can be a boon most of the time because from a very young age, you get fame. Those fortunate kids get fame right before they step into the world. 

Among those celebrity children, Banks Violet Bair is one of them. She is the daughter of the famous actress & singer Hilary Duff and musician Matthew Koma.

Who is Banks Violet Bair? 

Banks Violet Bair was born on October 25, 2018, in Los Angeles, United States of America. As mentioned before, her mother is a famous actress and her father is a musician. As for siblings, he has a stepbrother Luca Cruz. 

Her mom Duff, however, stole the limelight as the title character on the Lizzie McGuire Disney Channel series. Hilary is also wildly popular for her appearance in the movies like The Lizzie McGuire Movie (2003) and A Cinderella Story (2004). 

Not only in acting but her mother also laid her hands in the music field as she released several pop albums such as Metamorphosis (2003), her debut album, was triple-platinum. 

On the other hand, Banks' father Matthew is a popular music producer and singer. He also has released numbers of albums. 

Banks Violet Bair's Parents Dating Life

Banks' parents Hilary and Matthew started dating since 2017. They have an on-off relationship.

Well, the love birds met back in 2015 while collaboration on the Lizzie McGuire album, Breathe In Breathe Out. Two years after their meeting, they finally began their affair but separated soon in March 2017.

Months later in October 2017, the pair again came together. Moreover, on 9th May 2019, Hilary posted a picture of her and her partner where she was showing off a ring. She captioned the picture, ''He asked me to be his wife♥️''

The picture and the caption show that the couple is ready to get married.

Banks' Mother (Hilary)'s Past Affairs And Marriage

Before getting into the relationship with Matthew, Hilary was in a relationship with Jason Walsh. But the relationship did not last long and broke up soon only after five months.

Prior to the relationship, the star was married to Mike Comrie on 14th August 2010. Her ex-spouse is a professional hockey player for the National Hockey League's Pittsburgh Penguins. 

Two years after their conjugal life, the former soulmates welcome their first kid, Luca Cruz Comrie.

Like every relationship, they also had some hard times. The sparks started fading. Later, in 2015, Duff field the divorce due to her spouse's  aforementioned tabloid scandal. Following year, in January 2016, their divorce was finalized.

Banks Violet Bair's Father Dating History 

Just like Hillary Duff, Matthew also has a fair share of the past relationship. Her father was first in a relationship with Emily Rebecca Kinney for some time. The couple began to see each other in a very low-key way and just like that finished their relationship. 

Matthew similarly was in a loving relationship with Carly Rae Jepsen after finishing stuff with Emily. In 2012, the former pair began dating. After three years of dating, the couple decided to part their ways and broke up mutually in 2015. 

Banks' Guardians' Net Worth & Salary 

Well, a ten months infant can barely speak a word or two. In that case, Banks' has no source of income to earn any kind of money. However, her mother Duff has earned a really good amount of money from her successful career. Her net worth is $25 million as of 2019. Duff's main source of income is her acting career, music career, ads, and commercials. 

On the other hand, her father's net worth is around $6 million. Similarly, Koma's main source of income is his music career. 

At present, Hillary launched a new clothing brand, Little Moon Society, with business partner Jessica Frank.
