A Deep Dive: What Is Danica Patrick Doing Now?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, March 20, 2024

By Jocelyn Alano June 20, 2024 14:42

credit. Kevin D. Liles/ USA Today Sports

Danica Patrick, a trailblazing figure in the world of professional motorsports, has made waves not only on the race track but also in various other ventures. Let's delve into what Danica Patrick is up to now, her professional endeavors, and her passions beyond the racing circuit.

 

Racing Career and Transition

Danica's racing career began at a young age when her parents bought her a go-kart, igniting her passion for the sport. She went on to win numerous regional and national go-kart titles before making a bold move to compete in the demanding world of European road racing at the age of 16. Her racing achievements include leading 19 laps and finishing fourth in her first Indianapolis 500, making her the first woman to achieve such a feat. Moreover, she made history by winning a major-league open-wheel race in a North American series with her victory in the IndyCar Series Indy Japan 300 race. Transitioning to the NASCAR Cup Series, Danica continued to rewrite the history books. Her record-setting performance at the 55th Daytona 500 race, where she became the first woman to win a NASCAR Cup Series pole and finished in eighth place, solidified her status as a pioneering figure in the world of motorsports.

Beyond the Track

As her racing career concluded, Danica shifted her focus to her next chapter as an entrepreneur and business owner. She ventured into various domains, showcasing her versatility beyond the racing circuit.

Entrepreneurship and Business Ventures

Danica has embarked on diverse business ventures, including the launch of her own clothing line, "Warrior by Danica Patrick," and the publication of her fitness book, "Pretty Intense." Notably, she is the sole proprietor of Somnium Wine, owning a vineyard in California that produces high-quality wine.

 

Media and Broadcasting

In the realm of media and broadcasting, Danica has made significant strides. She has been a studio analyst for NBC's broadcasts of the Indianapolis 500 since 2019, showcasing her expertise and insight in the world of racing. Moreover, she has delved into podcasting, launching the "Pretty Intense" podcast, where she discusses success, spirituality, and consciousness with guests.

 

Endorsements and Philanthropy

Danica's influence extends to endorsement deals and philanthropy. She has appeared in advertising campaigns for notable brands such as GoDaddy, Chevrolet, and Coca-Cola. Additionally, she has been a celebrity spokeswoman for various causes, including the DRIVE4COPD awareness campaign for chronic obstructive pulmonary disease.

Personal Endeavors and Hobbies

Beyond her professional pursuits, Danica is known for her passion for good food, great wine, and fitness. She has been actively involved in promoting health-conscious lifestyles and has collaborated with various brands to advocate for noble causes, such as campaigning for No Kid Hungry and raising awareness of COPD.

In the End

Danica Patrick's journey from the race track to her multifaceted endeavors as an entrepreneur, media personality, and advocate reflects her unwavering determination and resilience. As she continues to make an impact in various domains, her story serves as an inspiration to many, showcasing the power of pursuing one's passions beyond the confines of a singular career. Danica's legacy extends far beyond her racing achievements, and her ongoing ventures exemplify the spirit of versatility and determination. As she embarks on this new chapter, Danica Patrick's unwavering spirit and dedication continue to inspire and captivate audiences worldwide.
