Travis Kelce and His Girlfriend: The Power Couple Making Us Swoon

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, September 8, 2024

In the summer of 2023, Travis Kelce made waves across the celebrity gossip-sphere when he publicly revealed he was officially dating Kayla Nicole again.

In addition to her work as a journalist and television host, Kayla is also a successful model and entrepreneur who owns a company and has a large following on YouTube. 

She has worked hard for much of her success, making it clear that she has amassed quite an impressive collection of assets over her career. From the looks of it, Travis was more than willing to welcome her back into his life with open arms. It’ll be interesting to see where their relationship will take them in the upcoming months!

After their reunion, the couple has been dating for a while and routinely share photos of one another on social media. In addition, Kayla has been seen at a number of Kelce’s games, demonstrating her support for the star tight end.

TMZ confirmed Kayla and Travis’ relationship in May of 2017. The pair started talking months prior, though, which Kayla confirmed via Instagram. 

In March 2022, Kayla did an “Ask Me Anything” on her Instagram Story and fielded a question about the beginning of her relationship with Travis. 

Together, Kayla and Travis have attended a number of high-profile occasions over the years, such as the ESPY Awards and the Kids’ Choice Sports Awards. She was present to support Travis and the Chiefs when they won the 2020 Super Bowl.

Kayla and Telvis Broke up In 2020

In August 2020, Travis Kelce and Kayla Nicole ended their relationship. After three years of dating, the unexpected and mysterious breakup perplexed fans and supporters.

Then, as if to remove all traces of her relationship with Travis, Kayla erased every photo of herself and the NFL player together from her social media accounts.

Many allegations about the athlete cheating on Kayla spread, but Kelce refuted them and clarified that it was false information and not the cause of their breakup.

However, in August 2020, Travis Kelce split with Kayla Nicole, and their fans thought they were not dating anymore and their relationship had ended. But they got back together after two years.

The couple was present at the 2023 Super Bowl, where Kelce and the Kansas City Chiefs won the championship, and they are still together today. 

They routinely share photos of themselves on social media and appear to be dating. They seem to be quite content together; it seems like Kelce is deeply in love with her dream lady.

About Kayla Brown 

Kayla made her name though through hard work and dedication in her modeling and T.V professions. She was previously known as Kayla Nicole, but later, she changed her surname to Kayla Brown; the reason for changing her last name was unknown.

Kayla is the daughter of Roosevelt Brown and Robin Curry, and they were separated when she was too young. Moreover, her mother, Robin, has been remarried.

The separation of Kayla’s parents at a young age did not affect her, and she still maintained a great relationship with both of them and her stepfather. 

She has four siblings. Furthermore, Kayla has four siblings; one elder sibling and three younger-half siblings altogether. Till now, there has been no information on her older sibling. 

On the contrary, the names of her other siblings are Hayda Curry, Layla-Simone Curry, and Ayva Curry. They are the children of Robin Curry and her new husband. The beautiful Journalist is close to her family.
