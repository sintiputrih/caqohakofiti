Lee Young-eun - Bio, Age, Net Worth, Height, Married, Facts

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, September 28, 2024

Table of Biography

Lee Young-Eun is a South Korean actress and model. Lee Young-Eun has appeared in many Korean movies and drama series.

Early Life and Childhood

Lee Young-Eun was born Lee Young-Eun on August 9, 1982. Her name in Hangul is written as ‘이영은’ and in Hanja is written as ‘李令恩’. She is originally from Daegu, South Korea. Lee holds South Korean nationality. She is 41 years old and her birth sign is Leo.

There are no details regarding her parents, siblings, and other relatives on the internet. However, she surely grew up along with her parents with lots of love and care. Moreover, she attended Dongduk Women’s University of Broadcasting and Entertainment.

Career and Professional Life

Lee is an actress. She debuted as an actress in the year 2003 by appearing in the drama series ‘My Fair Lady Cha’ as Joo-mi. She is popular for starring in dramas like ‘Kikeable or Not’, ‘Obstetrics and Gynecology Doctors’, ‘While You Were Sleeping’, and ‘Can’t Stand Anymore’.

Some other drama series that she appeared in are ‘Nonstop 4’, ‘Full House’, ‘Hong Kong Express’, ‘ Don’t Worry’, ‘War of Money’, ‘The King and I’, ‘Star’s Lover’, ‘Once Upon a Time in Saengchori’, ‘To the Beautiful You’, ‘Can’t Stand Anymore’, ‘My Secret Hotel’, ‘The Shining Eun-Soo’, ‘Should We Kiss First,’ ‘Home for Summer,’ and many more.

Lee has also starred in many movies. In the year 2004, she starred in ‘Too Beautiful to Lie’. Similarly, in 2007, she appeared in ‘Going by the Book’. She also appeared in the 2008 movie ‘Summer Whispers’. In 2009, she was also featured in ‘Oh! My God 2’. Likewise, in 2012, she starred in ‘Doomsday Book’. She starred in the 2015 movie ‘Granny’s Got Talent.’

She has starred in different music videos like “Destiny”, “Thanks”, “A Memory of Love”, “You Said You Can Love Me”, and “Sad Love Waltz”. Besides all this, she has also appeared in a variety of shows. In 2003, she appeared in the show ‘Kang Ho-dong’s Soulmates’. Likewise, she also starred in the show ‘Happiness in ₩10,000: Happiness Ltd’ in 2008. And, in the year 2018, she appeared in ‘Running Man’ in episode 397.

Awards and Nominations

This talented personality has received many awards and nominations in her career. In the year 2007, she received a award of ‘New Star Award’ in the SBS Drama Awards for her work in ‘War of Money.’ In 2011, for the category ‘Excellence Award, Actress in a Drama’ in ‘the 19th Korean Culture and Entertainment Awards’ won an award for her work in While You Were Sleeping’.

Also for the same drama, she was nominated in the SBS Drama Awards for the category ‘Excellence Award, Actress in a Weekend/Daily Drama.’ In 2019, in the ’31st KBS Drama Awards’ she won the award Excellence Award, for Actress in a Daily Drama for her work in ‘Home For Summer’.

Similarly, in 2008, at the ‘2nd Korea Drama Awards’, she was nominated for ‘Netizen Popularity Award for her work in ‘Likeable or Not’. Also, in 2009, at the ’46th Grand Bell Awards’, she was nominated for ‘Best New Actress for starring in ‘Summer Whispers’.

Personal Life

Lee is a married woman. She is married to her husband Go Jung-ho. Go Jung-ho is a producer at cable channel jTBC. The two tied the knot on September 27, 2014, at the Hotel Shilla in Seoul. They are having a great time and are enjoying their life together. There is no other information related to their relationship. However, visiting her Instagram page, it seems like they have two kids. She has posted many pictures of her kids on her Instagram.
