Kingdom Hearts 3: 100 Acre Wood Lucky Emblem Locations

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, June 21, 2024
Recommended Videos

Kingdom Hearts 3 is rich in collectibles disseminated for its maps. There are treasure chests and secrets, but there is a new type of collectible: the Lucky Emblems. They are icons in the shape of Mickey Mouse perfectly integrated into the environment that must be found and photographed with the Gummiphone. They are found in all the nine main worlds.

In this Kingdom Hearts III Lucky Emblems guide, we list the locations of all the Lucky Emblems in the 100 Acre Wood. So, let’s get started without wasting any time.

100 Acre Wood Lucky Emblems Locations

100 Acre Wood | Lucky Emblem Location #1

  • In this small world, there are only three lucky emblems. To find the first climb on the small wooden bridge and look towards the trees. Three hedges will form the first lucky emblem.

100 Acre Wood | Lucky Emblem Location #2

  • Go round to the far side of the large pumpkin to find another lucky emblem.

100 Acre Wood | Lucky Emblem Location #3

  • The final lucky emblem in 100 Acre Wood is in Rabbit’s laundry bucket, to the left side of his house.

These are all the emblems you can find in the 100 Acre Wood world. Have fun and see you soon with the other emblems present in the other worlds.
