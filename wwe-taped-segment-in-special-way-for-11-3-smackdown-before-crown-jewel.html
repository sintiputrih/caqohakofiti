WWE Taped Segment In Special Way For 11/3 SmackDown Before Crown Jewel

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

WWE has one more SmackDown before Crown Jewel is upon us from Saudi Arabia. The company taped this week’s episode of the blue brand last week, but one segment wasn’t seen by the live crowd.

The SmackDown brand is promoting a few segments and matches, including Logan Paul and Rey Mysterio’s weigh-in segment. Fans who saw the show taped live last week had some questions, because they didn’t see that segment go down.

Sean Sapp noted behind Fightful’s paywall that they will air a weigh-in segment with Rey Mysterio and Logan Paul on SmackDown this week. That being said, the two did not tape this in front of the live crowd at all.

“The advertised Weigh-In between Logan Paul and WWE United States Champion Rey Mysterio was not taped in front of the live crowd.“

Ringside News provided full spoiler for SmackDown this week, but the report from Fightful also went on to verify the lineup for this week’s episode. They did not provide any spoilers, and you can check the lineup out below.

– LA Knight promo

– Kevin Owens vs. Austin Theory

– B-Fab, Street Profits, Logan Paul and Lashley backstage

– Karrion Kross, Shotzi and Scarlett are set for a backstage segment

– Charlotte & Shotzi vs. Chelsea Green & Piper Niven (non title)

– John Cena & Solo Sikoa face to face

– Donnybrook Rules: Brawling Brutes vs. Pretty Deadly

– Logan Paul & Rey Mysterio weigh in

– Bianca Belair vs. Bayley

It was also noted that, “Roman Reigns is set to interrupt LA Knight’s promo.” The main event is slated to go two segments as well.

We will have full coverage of everything WWE does as they make their way to Crown Jewel. This is an exciting time for WWE fans as the company wraps up a banner year during a booming period in the company’s history.
