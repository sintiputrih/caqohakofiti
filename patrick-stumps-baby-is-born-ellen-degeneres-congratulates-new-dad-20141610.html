Patrick Stump's Baby Is Born, Ellen DeGeneres Congratulates New Dad

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, March 13, 2024

Who needs a birth announcement when you've got Ellen DeGeneres to do the job for you?!

Fall Out Boy's Patrick Stump is the proud dad to a newborn baby boy, talk show host DeGeneres announced via Twitter on Wednesday, October 15.

Stump and his wife, Elisa Yao, only revealed their exciting pregnancy news last month, during an interview with 102.7 KIIS FM on Wednesday, Sept. 3.

And keeping news of his son's arrival similarly secret, Stump, 30, left it to DeGeneres, whose show he will be appearing on in a couple of weeks, to make his happy news public.

"It’s a boy! Actually, it’s a @FallOutBoy," DeGeneres wrote, sharing news of the couple's first-born, and an adorable snap of the baby. "Congrats @PatrickStump and welcome baby Declan! See y’all in 2 weeks!"

Talking before the birth, Stump revealed the pitter-patter of tiny feet is causing chaos for the “Centuries” hitmakers.
