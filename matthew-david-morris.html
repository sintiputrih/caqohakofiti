Matthew David Morris- Professional Career

Matthew David Morris is a well-known rapper, singer, dancer, and Instagram personality from the United States. He is best known for his rapping skills, but he is also an accomplished singer, songwriter, actor, and dancer. Similarly, thanks to his YouTube channel, Facebook page, Instagram, and Twitter profiles, he has a considerable internet following. He has also released several original songs, all of which are accompanied by high-quality music videos, in addition to covering songs by well-known singers such as Willow Smith, Ke$ha, Rihanna, and Justin Bieber.

Further, In 2010, when he was only 7 years old, he and his cousin filmed a YouTube video covering Justin Bieber’s “Eenie Meenie,” which they posted on May 31. It received over 500k views within a week of its release and acted as a springboard for his spectacular rise to prominence. On Billboard’s “Social 50 Chart” in 2012, his music video for “That’s the Way” was ranked 11th. As his reputation grew, MattyB began to produce songs with original lyrics. He released his first such song, a cover of “I Believe in You,” on June 22, 2010. Within a few months, he and Tyler Ward, a well-known singer-songwriter, worked on a cover of the song “Just the Way You Are.”

“Forever and Always,” his debut original song, was released on September 8, 2011. He continued to appear on television in the years after, appearing on shows like ‘Fox 5 News at 10,’ ‘Today,’ and ‘Dr. Phil,’ as well as on ‘WWE Raw,’ alongside Dwayne “The Rock” Johnson. During this time, he performed live at Turner Field, Six Flags Over Georgia, Centennial Olympic Park, and a Nascar event at Atlanta Motor Speedway. He also worked on several national advertisements. When he was 11 years old, he was signed by the digital network “Endemol Beyond USA.”
