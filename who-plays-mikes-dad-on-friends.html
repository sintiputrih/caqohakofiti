Who plays Mike's dad on Friends?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Gregory Martin Itzin was an American character actor of film and television, perhaps best known for his role as Charles Logan in the action thriller series 24.

Who plays Mike’s dad on Friends?

The role of mike’s dad on friends was portrayed by Gregory Itzin. in Friends he was Theodore Hannigan, the father of Mike Hannigan and the husband of Bitsy Hannigan. He and his wife Bitsy dislike Mike’s new girlfriend called Phoebe Buffay and try to get him to go out with somebody else.

In real life, Itzin was born in Washington, D.C. in The United States. He is the son of Evelyn Loretta (née Smith) his father and Martin Joseph Itzin his mother. He was born on the 20th of April 1948. He was currently 74 years old when he passed away. He died on the 8th of July 2022.

When he was in sixth grade, his family moved to Burlington, Wisconsin, where his father was mayor. Itzin originally intended to become a theatre actor, receiving training at the American Conservatory Theater in San Francisco. He acted on many stages across the country.

His Awards and Nominations are the Primetime Creative Arts Emmy Award for Outstanding Guest Actor in a Drama Series 2010 and Outstanding Supporting Actor In A Drama Series – 2006

He was married to his wife name Judie Itzin in the year 1979 together the couple are blessed with two children namely Wilke Itzin and Julia Itzin.
