Brad Knighton Boating Accident

The former MLS goalkeeper took to his Instagram handle to pay tribute to his late daughter. The authorities said the circumstances surrounding the accident that cost the life of 11-year-old Olivia are not clear at this time. New England Revolution player Brad Knighton posted a picture of his daughter on social media and linked it to an obituary. He captioned the post, “Rest in Peace Olivia Grace Knighton. 11-year-old Olivia is survived by her father Brad, mother Britney, and siblings Owen and Brooks. Brad Knighton is a former MLS player who played from 2007 to 2022.
