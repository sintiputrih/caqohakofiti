Ricardo Hoyos is a Canadian actor who started his acting career at the age of 11 with a stint on the TV show, The Jon Dore Television Show. He found mainstream fame with his main role in the teen drama series, Degrassi: The Next Generation. Such was his popularity with the loyal fans of Degrassi universe that he was again cast in the lead role in Degrassi: Next Class. He has a huge social media fan base with more than 200k followers on Instagram.
