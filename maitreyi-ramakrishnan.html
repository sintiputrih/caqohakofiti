Early Life and Childhood

Maitreyi Ramakrishnan was born in Mississauga, Canada, on December 30, 2001. She is 22 years old and is under the birth sign, Capricorn. She holds Canadian nationality. Likewise, she is from the Srilankan Tamil family. However, she refers to herself as a Tamil Canadian. Moreover, Ramakrishnan was born and raised in Mississauga, Ontario.

Her father’s name is Ram Selvarajah and her mother is Kiruthiha Kulendiren. Her father is an IT Specialist and her mother works in the Marketing Field. Likewise, she has a brother and his name is Vishwaa Ramakrishnan. Moreover, her family is of Hindu Tamil descent from Sri Lanka. However her family came to Canada as refugees after the civil war in Sri Lanka. Likewise, she has also said that she identifies herself as a Tamil Canadian and not Sri Lankan due to the war’s impact on Sri Lankan Tamil civilians.

Regarding her educational background and qualifications,  she graduated from Meadowvale Secondary School. At the moment, she is studying at York University in Toronto for her further studies. However, the name of the high school she attended is not available.

Career and Professional Life

Maitreyi Ramakrishnan began acting in school plays in Grade 10. Likely, she was a die-hard fan of comics and wanted to pursue her career as an animator for comic creation. However, she changed her mind last year at Meadowvale Secondary School. Eventually, she got the role in “Never Have I Ever” less than a year after that.

Moreover, she deferred her acceptance to the theatre program at York University in Toronto. However, she had initially planned to attend the fall after she graduated high school, so she could shoot “Never Have I Ever” in Los Angeles.

She plays the lead role of Devi Vishwakumar in the rom-com Netflix series “Never Have I Ever”. And, in 2019, she was also chosen by Mindy Kaling out of 15,000 candidates that applied for an open casting call for the show. Moreover, she created her audition tape at a local library by using her mother’s camera.

Eventually, she was asked to send four more videos, before having a screen test in Los Angeles and being offered the role. Ramakrishnan was just 17 at the time of her casting. And she had not yet held a job or acted professionally. However, her casting received significant media attention, especially in Canada. She especially gathered attention due to the nature of the open casting call and for her Tamil Canadian identity.
