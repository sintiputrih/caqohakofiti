Is Vanderpump Rules Star Tom Schwartz Really Dating Jo Wenberg?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, February 15, 2024

Tom Schwartz, a famous actor in Hollywood, has spoken up about his personal life! Currently, there are numerous allegations about a relationship between the Vanderpump Rules star and Jo Wenberg, a hairstylist.

Jo Wenberg and Tom Schwartz are they dating? Find out the answer to the aforementioned query and see what the reality TV star had to say about hairstylist Jo Wenberg by reading on.

Are Tom Schwartz and Jo Wenberg dating?

The Essentially Stassi star told Us Weekly in their Hollywood hotspot, Schwartz & Sandy’s, during a joint interview with Tom Sandoval, “Jo is a kickass hairstylist. I believe she has a lovely soul.

Tom revealed to Scheana Shay in this week’s episode of Vanderpump Rules on Bravo that he had spent the previous night “hanging out” with Wenberg, who has routinely collaborated with the characters of the programme since 2015.

Schwartz denied ever having a hookup with Jo at the time. “We’re not living together and we’re not dating either,” he stated when asked about his connection with Wenberg at the time. “But she’s just someone I deeply love and she’s a really wonderful human being, so we’re just hanging around,” he said.

Bravo cameras caught Tom and Shay talking about Wenberg in July 2022. But, the two of them were spotted in a video from Raquel Leviss’ Instagram Stories from a concert in Los Angeles that December, leading some of her followers to believe that they might be more than just pals.

Tom Schwartz is not ready for another relationship right now

After his breakup with Katie Maloney, the co-founder of The Schwartz & Sandy’s said to the media outlet Us Weekly that he is in no way prepared for another romance. He had a brief affair with Raquel Leviss while season 10 was being made.

Tom said, “I recently ended a 12-year relationship. I am unable to be committed and I am unable to enter another relationship. I’m kind of consumed with, like, business and my family, and I’m kind of falling in love with myself all over again, so it wouldn’t be fair to the other person.

When did Tom Schwartz and Katie Maloney split?

For those of you who are unaware, let us inform you that Tom and Katie, who had been married for five years, announced their separation in the spring of 2022. The following October, their divorce was finally completed.

Katie said on a recent episode of “Pump Rules” that Schwartz’s commitment to his new bar contributed to their breakup. I’m trying to be excited for Tom, but this bar has a really problematic past,” she confessed in a private setting.

It severely strained our relationship, she continued. It simply had a lot more influence on my life than I had anticipated, and without my consent.
