Hyun Bin Height, Weight, Age, Girlfriend, Biography, Family &amp; More

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, January 29, 2024

Hyun Bin (born September 25, 1982) is a South Korean Former Marine Corps, Actor, Singer, and Famous Personality from Seoul. He first garnered fame after portraying a role in “My Name is Kim Sam-soon” in 2005.

He portrayed one of the major characters of Hyun Jin-heon in the TV series alongside Kim Sun-a. In fact, Bin won the Top Excellence Award at the MBC Drama Awards. Furthermore, Hyun then starred in the 2008 TV program “Worlds Within” directed by Pyo Min-soo and Kim Kyu-Tae.

The actor performed one of the leading roles as Jung Ji-oh along with actors like Song Hye-Kyo, Um Ki-Joon, Seo Hyo-rim, Kim Kap-soo, etc. His next performance as Kim Joo-won in “Secret Garden” (2010–2011) also appreciated by fans a lot.

Career, Movies, TV Shows & Net worth

The 37-years-old actor entered the acting world in 2003 and made debut with a show “Bodyguard”. He played a very small role as a stalker in the show. Later, he appeared in various projects such as Nonstop 4, Ireland, Nonstop 5, and Friend, Our Legend.

Meanwhile, Hyun also launched his profession in the movies after acting in the youth sports movie “Spin Kick” in 2004 as Lee Min-Gyu. His first film as a lead actor was “A Millionaire’s First Love” that came out in 2006.

Hyun Bin’s other acting projects are “I Am Happy”, “Friend, Our Legend”, “Come Rain, Come Shine”, “Late Autumn”, and much more. In fact, the artist received international fame with his role as Hoon in the Late Autumn movie.

Personal Life, Girlfriend & Wife

The young handsome hunk launched a romantic affair with his then-girlfriend “Hwang Ji-Hyun” back in 2006. She is a Korean pop singer and actress. The pair first met each other during a shoot and soon went on a date.

Thir break up was announced by KBS World Radio in a published work in June 2007. They parted their ways sometime in March 2007. Later, the actor began dating his next love interest Song Hye-Kyo (actress) in 2008.

Furthermore, the couple first met on the set of the comedy TV program “Worlds Within” the same year. They even announced their love life publicly. But the couple’s separation announced by their respective managers.

As per the source, Hyun also had romantic links with his ex-partner “Kang So-ra” from December 2016 to December 2017. Kang’s agency revealed about their affair in a statement.

However, they maintain their personal life very private and broke up because of work restraints. From 2018 to 2019, the singer had a romantic relationship with his co-star “Son Ye-jin”. As of January 2020, Bin is not seeing anyone but we assume he is focusing on his career.
