See Bella Hadid Pack on PDA in Blue Bikini on St. Barts Holiday

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, March 23, 2024

Fresh off a round of vintage red-carpet looks at the 75th Cannes Film Festival, Bella Hadid has swapped her gowns for bikinis for a sun-kissed holiday in St. Barts.

The supermodel was spotted during a loved-up outing at the Eden Rock hotel, where she was accompanied by her boyfriend, art director Marc Kalman.

Hadid looked radiant in a blue bikini top and matching shorts, which she wore underneath a button-up. She opted for flashy accessories, wearing multiple gold bangles up to her elbows and layers of gold necklaces. Meanwhile, Kalman kept it casual in black board shorts.

Photos show the couple having a PDA-filled outing as they sunbathe, swim, and take pictures of each other.

MEGA

Prior to arriving in St. Barts, Hadid and Kalman embarked on a brief getaway to Antibes, France. There, the Kin Euphorics partner offered up more style inspiration for summer. For one outing, she wore a strapless white monokini with waist cutouts underneath ivory board shorts, accessorizing with white-and-tan Adidas sneakers, a Prada shoulder bag, and gold jewelry. Later, she changed into a navy blue bikini top and a patterned cover-up.

Hadid's beachy looks come after she swept this year's Cannes Film Festival in various vintage designer pieces. Styled by Law Roach, Hadid appeared at Cannes in vintage Versace, opting for a dramatic black gown from the house's spring 1987 collection for her first red-carpet walk and later changing into a dress with hip cutouts from the 2001 ready-to-wear runway. She was also seen attending a gala in a vintage black-and-white gown from Chanel.
