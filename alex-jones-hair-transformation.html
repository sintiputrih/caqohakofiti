The One Show's Alex Jones unveils new look: see transformation

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Alex Jones has switched up her hairstyle!

    Alex Jones decided to experiment with her hairstyle on Tuesday, ditching her usual look for something a little more edgy. presenter took to her Instagram page to reveal her brand new cut, writing in the caption: "The fringe is back. Thanks to hair wizard Esther @beepershair. A much need cut and colour. Last had it done in April." The TV star also revealed that her beauty regime has taken a back seat ever since she became a mother, adding: "I'm a disgrace, I know, especially as I'm on the telly, but I'm afraid haircuts and colour have fallen right to the bottom of the list of priorities over the last 9 months!! So nice to finally have it done!"
