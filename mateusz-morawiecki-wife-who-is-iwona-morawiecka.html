Mateusz Morawiecki Wife: Who is Iwona Morawiecka?

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, August 28, 2024

Iwona Morawiecka is well known as the wife of Polish economist, historian, and politician who has served as prime minister of Poland since 2017 Mateusz Morawiecki.

Iwona and Morawiecki married for long time, through their marriage the couple share four children; Aleksandra Morawiecka, Magdalena Morawiecka, Jeremiasz Morawiecki, and Ignacy Morawiecki.

Aside from these facts, Iwona other details are not available.

However, Iwona’s husband Mateusz Morawiecki is a Polish economist, historian, and politician who has served as prime minister of Poland since 2017.

He previously served in the cabinet of prime minister Beata Szydło as deputy prime minister from 2015 to 2017, as Minister of Development from 2015 to 2018, and as Minister of Finance from 2016 to 2018. Prior to his political appointment, Morawiecki had an extensive business career.

In December 2021, German Chancellor Olaf Scholz came to Warsaw for talks with Morawiecki.

They discussed Poland’s dispute with the EU over the rule of law, the long-term EU climate policies and the Nord Stream 2 gas pipeline, which would bring Russian gas to Germany and bypass Poland.
