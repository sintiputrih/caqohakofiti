Mills Watson was born on July 10, 1940 in Oakland, California, USA as William Oscar Watson. He is an actor, known for Papillon (1973), Cujo (1983) and The Misadventures of Sheriff Lobo (1979). He was previously married to Caroline Mary Mason. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
