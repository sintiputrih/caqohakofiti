Ryan Garcia’s Early Life and Boxing Career

Ryan Garcia, born on August 8, 1998, in Victorville, California, had an early start in the world of boxing. At the age of seven, he embarked on his boxing journey, showcasing his natural talent and passion for the sport. Garcia quickly rose through the ranks, becoming a 15-time national amateur champion with an impressive record of 215 wins and 15 losses.

Recognizing his potential, Garcia turned professional at the age of 17 and signed with Golden Boy Promotions. Since then, he has captivated audiences with his electrifying performances in the ring. Garcia has remained undefeated in all 23 of his fights, proving his skill and determination.

Throughout his career, Garcia has faced formidable opponents and emerged victorious. His notable wins include fights against Luke Campbell and Romero Duno, which have solidified his position as one of the top-ranked lightweight boxers in the world.
