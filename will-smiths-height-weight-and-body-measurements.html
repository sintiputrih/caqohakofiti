Will Smith’s Weight

Willard’s weight is a pretty juicy topic. Like most actors and actresses in Hollywood, it’s not strange to add a few pounds or drop a few for a movie role. Be that as it may, the actor maintained his good “shape” for a long time.

He looks ripped when he needs to and toned when he needs to. In the movie ‘Ali,’ the actor got bigger and built more muscle, while he lost weight for his role in ‘I am Legend’ and his recent movie ‘Suicide Squad,’ Will went the extra mile and featured an eight-pack, because six-pack is so much last season.

The actor currently weighs 82kg which is pretty amazing given his height and build. As they often say here, it doesn’t happen overnight, again the dynamic duo of “hard work” and “compromise” has to come into play. The actor undergoes intense workout routines throughout his week, combining cardio-training and weight training, in addition to following a fairly refined diet that comforts him in his training.

He sticks to a high-protein, high-carb diet that helps him build muscle and lose fat; and just to clarify that, he eats healthy carbs like vegetables and whole grains, so if you plan on emulating him, put away baked and fried foods.

With these celebrities, you can hardly keep up, but leading a healthy life, no matter how big, is a necessity.
