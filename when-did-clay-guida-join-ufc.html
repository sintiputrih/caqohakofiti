When did Clay Guida join UFC?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, February 23, 2024

By Jason Bolton September 26, 2023 08:54

Clay Guida, the American professional mixed martial artist, joined the UFC in 2006. Since then, Guida has become a fan favorite and a must-see fighter in the lightweight division. Known for his exciting fighting style and relentless energy, Guida has consistently put on thrilling performances in the octagon, earning him the title of "must see TV". With five Fight of the Night bonuses and two victories under his belt, Guida has proven himself to be a formidable opponent in the UFC. As he gears up for his 45th career fight against Tatsuya Kawajiri at UFC Fight Night 39 in Abu Dhabi, Guida remains as enthusiastic and energetic as ever, showing no signs of slowing down. Despite a stint in the featherweight division, Guida is open to returning to the lightweight division and believes he still has a lot left in his career. Guida even expressed interest in facing Anthony Pettis, the current lightweight champion, again, citing his previous victory over him. At 32 years old, Guida remains confident in his abilities and believes he can continue to compete at a high level for many more years. With his passion, skill, and determination, Guida is undoubtedly a force to be reckoned with in the UFC lightweight division.
