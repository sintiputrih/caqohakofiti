Jaqueline Jauregui Bio, Daniele Uditi Wife, Age, Job, IG

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 24, 2024

Best in Dough’s head judge, Daniele Uditi is the LA-based master pizzaiolo working his magic at Pizzana. Customers from afar come to his shop to taste Daniele’s prized pies. But amongst them, living the dream was Jaqueline Jauregui, Daniele’s wife — who had access to the judge’s bakery creations 24/7.

Keep reading this Jaqueline Jauregui Bio to learn more about her.

Meet Jaqueline Jauregui, Daniele Uditi Wife

Jaqueline Jauregui first met Daniele Uditi in 2010 in California.

At the time, Daniele had just traveled to the United States to work as a consulting chef for various Italian restaurants in the Los Angeles area. But what was meant to be a six-month stint turned into a permanent move, as he fell in love with the beautiful Jaqueline.

Fast forward to 2 years of dating, the lovebirds then exchanged vows on June 12, 2012.

They were married for over a decade, at the time of writing this article.

Prior to meeting Jaqueline, Daniele honed his culinary skills under the guidance of chef Rosanna Marziale at the Michelin-starred Le Colonne restaurant in Caserta, Italy.

However, the Best in Dough judge has been involved in the pastry business ever since he was 12. Thanks to his chef mother, bread-maker aunt, and pastry chef grandfather, Daniele grew up cleaning the pizza ovens and baking loaves of bread at the family bakery before school each morning.

How Many Kids Do Jaqueline Jauregui And Daniele Uditi Have?

Jaqueline Jauregui and Daniele Uditi share a son named Kuno. They welcomed him in 2017, and watching him grow up has been a blessing for the pair.

According to Daniele, raising Kuno around incredible food just as he did was delightful. And the fact that Kuno had a creative eye (he once topped his homemade pizza with Cheetos), was a cherry on the top.

“Kuno loves making a mess, like me when I was a kid. Anytime I make pizza, he’s in with his hands in the sticky stuff. And he’s actually very good at stretching pizza,” the judge praised his son.

Furthermore, Daniele even often featured Kuno on his TikTok videos.

Overall, Jaqueline and Daniele are proud parents. On father’s day 2020, Daniele even took it to his IG to wish himself and every father out there — a “Happy Father’s Day.”

Jaqueline Jauregui Age

Jaqueline Jauregui was 37 years of age in 2022.

She is over 4 years younger than her husband Daniele.

Jaqueline Jauregui Job

Jaqueline Jauregui is a payroll specialist.

As of 2022, she was working as a Sr. payroll analyst at Canoo for 2 years. There, her mission was to bring EVs to everyone. Unlike anything on the road today, Canoo developed breakthrough electric vehicles which reinvented the automotive landscape with “bold innovations in design, pioneering technologies, and a unique business model that spans all owners in the full lifecycle of the vehicle.”

Before that, Jaqueline worked as a “payroll assistant” at YogaWorks Inc. for 2 ½ years, “office manager” at Socal Spine Inc (Nov 2011 – Nov 2013), “administrative assistant” at Player One Amusement Group (May 2010 – Nov 2011), and “office assistant” at Ventura County Community College District (Feb 2005 – May 2010).

Besides this, Jaqueline also has been involved in her husband’s business “Danieleuditi.org” ever since Dec 2020. There, she handled the finance. But unlike the kind of food business that you’re thinking of, “Danieleuditi.org” sold books where one can learn all the skills they’ll ever need to become a great baker from the comfort of one’s own kitchen.

“Chef Daniele Uditi walks you through every step of the process with helpful photos and videos to guide you along the way,” their bio read.

The last we checked, “Ricettario Vol. 1 – Sourdough – Downloadable PDF Version” cost $15, and “Ricettario Vol. 1 – Sourdough – SPECIAL LIMITED PRINT EDITION” (now sold out) cost $25.

A percentage of all their purchases was also donated to World Central Kitchen to help restaurants and restaurant workers during the 2020 pandemic.

As for Jaqueline’s education, she received her associate’s degree in accounting and management from Ventura College in 2006, and later received her bachelor’s degree in global studies from the University of California, Santa Barbara in 2009.

Mentioning her distinct features, Jaqueline has brown eyes, an oval face, and naturally dark hair (which she prefers blonde).
