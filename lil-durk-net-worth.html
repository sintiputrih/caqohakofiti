Lil Durk Net Worth: Lifestyle &amp; Cars

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, February 19, 2024

Chicago-born American rapper and singer Lil Durk’s net worth is $3 million as of 2023.

Durk is the lead member and co0founder of the record label Only the Family (OTF).

He was signed to Def Jam Recordings after releasing his Signed to the Streets mixtapes series in 2013-2014.

He was also able to garner a cult following with the release.

With Def Jam, he released his first studio album in 2015 entitled Remember My Name, and in 2016 he released Lil Durk 2X. Nonetheless, Def Jam dropped the deal with Durkin 2018.

Lil Durk debuted on the Billboard Hot 100 with his hit single “Viral Moment” in April 2020, a song from his fifth studio album released in 2020 named “Just Cause Y’all Wait for 2.”

He collaborated with fellow rappers Drake, Lil Baby, and others releasing some of the commercially successful singles of his career.

His hit singles such as “3 Headed Goad”, “Backdoor,” “The Voice,” “Laugh Now Cry Later,” and “Back in Blood” were well received by the fans and critics.

His joint album released in 2021 with Lil Baby “The Voice of the Heroes” became his first to debut atop Billboard 200.

Lil Durk: Early Life

Lil Durk, originally Durk Derrick Banks, was born on October the 19th, 1992, in Chicago, Illinois, United States.

Since his father was imprisoned when he was just 7 months old, he grew up with a great deal of responsibility at home.

His family’s economic condition was so bad that they did not even have enough food at home.

He started putting content on social media platforms such as YouTube and Myspace and grew fond of the idea of being a rapper as the fan base started to expand.

He dropped out of Paul Robeson High School to join a street gang in Chicago Black Disciples.

Lil Durk became a father at the early age of 17, after which he started taking his career more seriously.

As one might expect, Durk started getting gin trouble after joining a gang. He was charged with firearm possession with a defaced serial number in October 2011, of which he pleaded guilty to reduce his time in prison.
